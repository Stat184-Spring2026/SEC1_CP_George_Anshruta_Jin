# Retatrutide vs Placebo — GLP-1 Analysis
Exploring the effects of the new GLP-1 drug Retatrutide by comparing treatment participants to placebo participants and benchmarking against other GLP-1 drugs.
## Overview
We plan to explore the recent GLP-1 drug Retatrutide by analyzing datasets of users taking Retatrutide vs placebo participants to make multiple inferences and discover the different effects of Retatrutide compared to the placebo. We will also compare Retatrutide against other GLP-1 drugs to see how it stacks up in terms of effectiveness and side effects. The goal is to produce a clear, reproducible analysis that tells a real story about whether (and how much) Retatrutide actually works.

## Current Plan
Our meta goals for this project:
1. Strengthen our understanding of causal inference in clinical trial data, specifically treatment vs placebo comparisons.
2. Produce a reproducible data analysis so anyone with our repo can run it and follow along.
In practice, this means:
- Cleaning and exploring the Retatrutide and placebo datasets
- Running statistical comparisons between groups
- Comparing Retatrutide outcomes against other GLP-1 drugs
- Documenting every step so results can be reproduced
See PLAN.md for the detailed plan.
